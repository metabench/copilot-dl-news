SpaceX Starship Completes Historic Mars-Ready Test Flight

By Marcus Webb December 21, 2025

SpaceX has successfully completed its most ambitious Starship test flight yet, demonstrating all the key capabilities needed for future Mars missions.

The vehicle reached orbital velocity before executing a controlled deorbit burn and splashing down in the Pacific Ocean. Watch the full video above for complete coverage of this historic launch.
