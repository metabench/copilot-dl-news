Governor Declares State of Emergency Amid Winter Storm

Staff Report Posted 3:45 AM ET - December 22, 2025 DEVELOPING

This is a developing story. Check back for updates.

The governor has declared a state of emergency for 15 counties as a major winter storm bears down on the region, bringing heavy snow and dangerous ice.

The National Weather Service has issued blizzard warnings through Tuesday morning, with snowfall totals expected to reach 18-24 inches in some areas.

Travel is strongly discouraged. Multiple highways have been closed and dozens of flights have been cancelled.

Power outages are being reported across the affected area. Utility crews are on standby.
